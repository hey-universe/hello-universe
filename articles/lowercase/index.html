everything is lowercase

nov 10, 2024
matt harris

you'll notice that everything here is lowercase. i decided to remove the caps lock and shift keys from my workflow.

i find it soothing for my mental health and the simplicity and visual continuity are a beautiful combination.

there are cases where an occasional uppercase letter is necessary like when a coding language requires it. hopefully we can see that change in time. maybe the keys on our keyboards will be lowercase someday too like they should be in their pre-shifted state. after we all convert, maybe we can use the caps lock key for fun sound effects.

we won't be able to convince everyone to switch right away, but wherever you can, i hope you'll join the #lowercase movement.
